Earthquake Safety Guide

Be prepared. Stay safe. Protect lives.

What to Do During an Earthquake

  • Drop to your hands and knees to prevent falling.
  • Cover your head and neck with your arms or crawl under sturdy furniture.
  • Hold on to furniture to stay covered and protected.
  • If outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings, trees, and power lines.
  • Stay indoors if you are inside. Avoid running outside.
